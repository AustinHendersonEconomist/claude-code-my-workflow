**REPLACE: Feldstein pp. 437–442**

The Feldstein excerpt is from a 1990s US health economics textbook. It is US-centric, dated, and hard to obtain. Replace with:

> Papanicolas, I., Woskie, L.R. & Jha, A.K. (2018). Health care spending in the United States and other high-income countries. *JAMA*, 319(10), 1024–1039. DOI: [10.1001/jama.2018.1150](https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.2018.1150)
